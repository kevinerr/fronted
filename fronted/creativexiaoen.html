  Hong Kong-Zhuhai-Macao Bridge:
  After five years of planning and nine years of construction, it has been completed for 14 years. The Hong Kong-Zhuhai-Macao Bridge, with a total length of about 55kilometers, crosses the ocean, connects Hong Kong to the east, and connects Zhuhai and Macau to the west. It is the longest sea-crossing bridge in the world. It is also the first large-scale cross-sea traffic project jointly built by Guangdong, Hong Kong and Macao.
  In the future, the time and space between Hong Kong, Zhuhai and Macao will be greatly shortened. From Hong Kong to Zhuhai and Macau, the speed will be shortened from 3 hours to 45 minutes. The western part of the Pearl River Delta will be included in the three- hour drive to Hong Kong .

    ———— What is the significance of the Hong Kong-Zhuhai-Macao Bridge? What are the real effects?

  After the opening of the Hong Kong-Zhuhai-Macao Bridge, the most intuitive impact on Hong Kong, Macau and Zhuhai is that the exchanges between the three places will be more convenient. Zhuhai is a beautiful coastal city. Macau is an international tourism and leisure center of the world. Hong Kong is an internationally renowned free port and international financial center. The Hong Kong-Zhuhai-Macao Bridge connects the three places together, and it is a collection of tourism, leisure and shopping. The central part of the Dawan District, which is integrated into business, has been initially presented. In the future, Zhuhai people are fully qualified to take the lead in realizing the vision of living in Zhuhai, leisure in Macau, and studying and working in Hong Kong.
