  On 29th October, 2015, Xi Jinping, the general secretary of the party's 18 speech at the Fifth Plenary Session of the Second Plenary Session of the distinctive proposed innovation, coordination, green, open, shared development concept. The new development concept is in line with China's national conditions and conforms to the requirements of the times. It has great guiding significance for solving development problems, enhancing development momentum and strengthening development advantages.

          —— Xi Jinping "The new development concept is the baton, traffic lights" ,NetEase



   “Innovation” refers to the creating or creation of new ones. It’s originated from "Southern History, Posthumous, Songsizu Yin Shuyi". This innovative thinking. During the Warring States Period, Shangyang moved from Wei to Qin , and proposed a set of reform strategies such as abandoning wellfields, paying attention on agricultural, awarding military and unifing measurements to achieve a new set of reforms, so that the country’s economy has also developed and the country’s strength has gradually grown. Today, China's development has entered a new era, and it still attaches great importance to innovation. General Secretary Xi Jinping pointed out during the deliberation of the Shanghai delegation of the Third Session of the 12th National People's Congress: "Innovation is the first driving force for development. Grasping innovation means grasping development, and innovation is seeking the future." This important statement made by our party is a new understanding of the role of scientific and technological innovation and of great significance for better implementing innovation-driven development strategies and building innovative countries.

  “Coordination” means that it is properly matched. From the forty-seventh chapter of Ming Feng Menglong's "East Zhou Lie Guo Zhi". The word "coordination" in the new development concept of the new era is aimed at poverty alleviation. We will strengthen the regional coordinated development, coordinated development, and common development, and ultimately achieve the goal of common prosperity. At the same time, the term "coordination" also refers to the overall planning, simultaneous implementation and coordinated development of environmental protection and economic construction and social development, and the unification of economic, social and environmental benefits. The economy is not developed at the expense of the environment, but people and nature live in harmony and promote each other.

  “Green” is synonymous with harmony, sustainability and nature. The ancients have already studied the concept of “green”, such as the idea of harmony between man and nature mentioned in the Tao Te Ching, and the sustainable development of Li Bing’s construction the Dujiangyan Water Conservancy Project and Li Shizhen’s Compendium of Materia Medica are all seeking peaceful coexistence between man and nature. Today, the term “green” in the new development concept is a way of economic growth and social development with the goal of efficiency, harmony and sustainability. The national environmental protection concept is deepening , environmental investment continues to increase , and environmental pollution control is effective , while steadily Promote ecological protection and ecological construction .

  “Openness” means “going out” and “bringing in”. In ancient China, Zheng He’s voyages to the West and Zhang’s ambassador to the Western Regions. To a certain extent, both of them were close to the economic exchanges and cultural exchanges between the East and the West and promoted the world civilization. The exchange of communication between the two also shows the strong national strength of China and the promotion of national prestige. Zhang Wei’s departure made the Western Region also open up the famous “Silk Road”, and Zheng He’s Xiayang opened up many overseas markets. Nowadays, modern society also needs to strengthen "openness". The "Belt and Road" in the 21st century is the concept and initiative of China's cooperation and development. It borrows the historical symbols of the ancient "Silk Road", holds high the banner of peaceful development, and actively develops and The economic partnership between the countries along the line will create a community of mutual trust, economic integration, cultural inclusion, and a community of responsibility for the community of destiny.

  " Share " , that is, using but not monopolying, share together. While the thought of share sweeping the globe, as early as in ancient China has devised a shared economic times - " Great Community " The best use of everything, people do their best, everyone settles down, is a blueprint for an ideal society. Nowadays, this is not only a kind of thinking, but also a way of life, gradually infiltrating into the layer of society, is a long-term gradual historical process. The maturity of the sharing concept from the sharing of material products to the spiritual products adapts to the changes of the main contradictions in the new era, contains the values of fairness and justice, and is conducive to the realization of the value of human freedom and all-round development.
